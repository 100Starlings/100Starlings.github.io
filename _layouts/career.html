We want to talk to you

We’re a small group of freelancers that have formed a digital agency / cooperative. We’ve been very successful at having more work than we can handle - and that is taking its toll on us.

That is why we’re looking for a number of people to join our ranks – web professionals who want to improve themselves, shape the form of the cooperative, and make an impact while working in an international team of skilled peers.

In doing so, you’ll get to work on challenging projects, learn from us or even lead the way.

What you’ll get - let’s start with flexibility on:

  • When – work no longer has to be a 9-to-5 chore, so if only working 3 days per week motivates you and fits your balance – that’s fine with us too.
  • Where – your choice – we have an office at Colabs Buda, but a lot of us practice remote working, from either London, Budapest, Keszthely, so it’s really up to you.
  • How – you can be a permanent employee, or work contract. At the end of the day, it’s the hours you put in, and impact you produce that matters, so if working contract under KATA is enough, we’ll help you make it happen. The cooperative is flexible enough to make it work for everyone’s interests.
  • Reward – we ensure that what you’ll be rewarded with is above market prices. If you excel and contribute to success, we allocate bonuses based on individual performance as a means of profit sharing.
  • Training – we run a conference and training budget, so you could be coming with us to CRAFT this year if you can attend. Or you could find the best way that suits your professional improvement.
  • Mentoring – we’re swamped with work, but if you want to learn, you can help us with that workload and skill up. That’s why you don’t have to be an expert, and if you are, you’ll find us easy to get along with and pleasure to work alongside.

A bit more about us

You can see our fledgling website at www.100Starlings.com – but we realise that doesn’t do justice to the talent of the people you’ll be working alongside, nor the projects and technical challenges you’ll either be cutting your teeth into, or shaping up solutions for. You might have noticed that we’ve worked with some big client names – they’re not the easiest to please, but we’ve always left satisfaction in our wake.

At least 4 of us have 10+ years experience in the web development field – primarily Ruby on Rails, but also Node.js, as well as JavaScript frameworks (Angular, Ember, etc). We also do mobile development – native iOS, Android, as well as hybrid Angular / Ionic. You’ll also find DevOps and UX & Visual design in our midst. Suffice to say, you’ll be exposed to much you could learn from.

If you join, you’ll also be dealing with clients directly – we believe all of us should be able to wear multiple hats, and quite often will architect, project manage, consult and advise.

Most of our clients are not based in Hungary, so it goes without saying – your English doesn’t have to be perfect, but you shouldn’t feel like a fish out of water if you find yourself on a trip abroad.

If you think you’re up for the challenge, let us know by emailing us at career@100starlings.com.
